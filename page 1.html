h2>Group life insurance

a group life insurance policy provides life cover protection to multiple individuals under single or master contract policy. The insurance company does not have to go through the tedious process of filling out applications or conduct medical tests for each applicant.

Third party motor insurance

facebook

Motor Third Party Liability Insurance (MTPL) ensures that damage to third party health and property caused by an accident for which driver and/or owner of the car were responsible is covered. A policy may be taken out by the owner of a vehicle or by a lawful possessor authorized by the owner on behalf of the owner.

Public building and buildings under construction

October 25, 2023. The Insurance Act 2003 made compulsory the insurance of Buildings Under Construction and Occupiers Liability for public buildings , with the objective to protect third parties for damage or loss of life that may arise in the course of construction or making use of the facility

Emplyer's liability

Employers' Liability Insurance covers the costs of employee claims for illness or injury caused by their work due to employers' negligence. For example: An employee is injured whilst operating machinery due to the employers' negligence. This may include poor machine maintenance, protective equipment, or training.

Healthcare professional insurance indemnity

It is purchased to protect a physician or health care institution from the financial risks-the liability-of practicing medicine. More specifically it protects the physician from the consequences of a patient's claim that he or she was injured as a result of the physicians' negligence.
